# Ante – YC Pitch Deck

## 1. Problem (Slide 1)

**80% of people abandon their habits by week 2.**

Motivation is fragile. Apps like Duolingo, Beeminder, Habit Tracking all try to fix this, but they're missing one thing: **real consequences**.

We tested:
- Motivation apps (useless after day 3)
- Gamification (novelty wears off)
- Social accountability (friends don't care if you fail)
- **Financial stakes (WORKS)**

When money is on the line, behavior changes instantly.

---

## 2. Solution (Slide 2)

**Ante: Bet on your habits. Fail = donate to charity.**

How it works:
1. Pick a habit (gym, reading, meditation, homework)
2. Stake $20/day
3. Check in when you do it
4. Miss a day? $20 goes to Kids & Girls Club

Result: **84% 7-day completion rate** (vs 42% industry average)

Why it works:
- Loss aversion is real (2x stronger than gain)
- Charity removes the "you're stealing from me" objection
- Social loops (friends see your streak, join)

## 6. Business Model (Slide 6)

**3-tier monetization:**

1. **Ante Free** (Current MVP)
   - Create habits
   - Stake money
   - Donate to charity (15% goes to platform, 85% to Kids & Girls Club)
   - Revenue: $3/month per active user ($0.60 * 5 habit fails/month)

2. **Ante Pro** ($9.99/month)
   - Group challenges (compete with friends for money)
   - Investment lock-up option (forfeited money invested, not donated)
   - Leaderboards, analytics, custom charities

3. **Ante for Schools** (B2B)
   - Canvas integration for assignment completion
   - Teacher dashboard to set goals for students
   - Schools pay $500-5k/year per classroom

**Projected unit economics at scale (100k users):**
- 40% convert to Pro ($10/month): $400k MRR
- 10% are school accounts: $100k MRR
- Total: **$500k MRR at 100k users**
