## 🚨 Red Flags to Avoid

❌ "Sign up here" – Nobody will click. They need to try instantly.
❌ "Download the app" – It's a web app. Just click the link.
❌ "Get motivated" – Boring. Lead with the money angle.
❌ Posting too much – Share once per Discord, once on Twitter. Don't spam.
