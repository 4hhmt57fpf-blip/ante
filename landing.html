Behavioral science

This is why it works.

🧠
Loss aversion — Kahneman & Tversky showed losses feel 2× as powerful as equivalent gains. Ante turns every potential missed habit into a loss, not a missed reward.
📋
Commitment devices — Pre-committing to a cost before the temptation arrives is the most effective habit-formation technique known. StickK's research showed 3× higher completion.
👥
Social accountability — Public commitments with stakes outperform private ones by 2.5×. When your friends can see your streak, quitting feels like failure twice over.
2×
Losses feel twice as bad as gains feel good
(Kahneman & Tversky, 1979)
