## Key Metrics to Track

**User Acquisition**
- Total sign-ups (target: 50)
- Source (friend, Discord, Twitter, etc.)
- Conversion rate (sign-up → create first habit)

**Engagement**
- Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Habits created per user
- Check-ins per user per day
- Streak distribution (median streak length)

**Retention**
- Day 1 retention (% who come back next day)
- Day 7 retention (% still active after 1 week)
- Churn rate (% who stop using)

**Monetization** (even at MVP)
- Total monthly donation amount
- Average donation per failed habit
- Projected annual charity donation

---

## Red Flags to Watch

- ❌ Users create habit but never check in = onboarding problem
- ❌ Users check in once then quit = motivation problem
- ❌ Users don't share with friends = social sharing not working
- ❌ >50% churn by day 3 = core mechanic not working
