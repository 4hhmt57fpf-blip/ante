Help
How does Ante work?
Stake money on a daily habit. Complete it (auto-verified by a connected app) and keep your money. Miss it and the stake locks into your Savings Vault.
How is a habit verified?
Each category links an app — Apple Health, Screen Time, Canvas, GPS, Kindle, or Duolingo — and reads the data automatically. No honor system.
What's the Savings Vault?
Missed stakes go into a locked vault — still your money, just inaccessible until you build the habit back.

🎓 Connect Canvas
Ante reads your assignments straight from your own Canvas account using a personal access token. The token stays on your device and is only ever sent to your school's Canvas.
How to get your token
1.  Open Canvas in a browser and log in.
2.  Go to Account → Settings.
3.  Scroll to Approved Integrations → click + New Access Token.
4.  Purpose: Ante. Leave the expiry blank → Generate Token.
5.  Copy the token (shown only once) and paste it below.
Your school's Canvas URL
Access token
School blocks tokens? (UMich does.) Use the Ante browser extension instead — it reads your assignments through your logged-in Canvas, no token needed.
1.  Chrome → chrome://extensions → turn on Developer mode → Load unpacked → select the canvas-extension folder in your Ante project.
2.  Open Canvas, click the Ante extension → Sync my Canvas to Ante.
3.  Come back and tap below.
🔒 Your Canvas data stays on your machine (browser + local Ante server) and is never sent anywhere else.
